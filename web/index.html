About this app

Stay connected with your campaigns, no matter where you are. The Meta Ads Manager app for iOS and Android lets you keep tabs on your ad campaigns and respond to performance changes quickly from your phone or tablet.

With the Meta Ads Manager app, you can:
• Monitor ad campaign performance with up-to-date metrics and charts
• Edit existing ads or create new ones directly from the app
• Review and approve ads on the go
• Receive important notifications about your accounts, campaigns, ad sets, and ads
• Manage your ad spending with access to billing information

You must have a Facebook account to use this app. Meta Ads Manager is available for advertisers in all countries.
